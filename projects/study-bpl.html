Study BPL is an app that enables you to find and choose spots at the Boston Public Library. It includes maps of each floor and tells you which room you are in, as well as what each room's functionality is. You can view the project on Invision here.

The project was inspired by my frustration in finding outlets and quiet spaces to study at the library. Although it is filled with beautiful rooms and spaces, it is hard to navigate the library and find which one is right for you.
