[ WILSON + LARSSON = WILLAR ]

Willar Design is a design collaboration between Julia Wilson and Vendela Larsson. We are currently in the stages of setting up the company and creating a brand presence for ourselves. We have begun to take on client work, but more as independent designers than under the company name. Once up and running, we will be taking on brand strategy projects in several types of media from print to mobile to web.
